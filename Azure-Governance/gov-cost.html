Blocks services that cause bill shock. Requires a Policy Exemption and Business Owner approval.

🚫 Blocked by default:
• Compute: GPU (N-series), High-RAM (M-series), HPC (H-series) VMs

New Resource Requests
Blocked immediately — "SKU blocked by finance policy. Contact IT-Ops for an exemption."
Existing Resources

Save
Monthly budget threshold — Alerts fire at 80% and 100% of threshold, notifying the assigned owner before overspend occurs.
📌 Portal steps:
  1. Azure Portal → Cost Management
  2. Select Subscription → Budgets → + Add
  3. Reset period: Monthly | Amount: e.g. 10,000 DKK
  4. Add alert at 80% and 100% → email: it-ops@company.com
  5. Click Create
NOT VIA POLICY
New Subscriptions
No automatic budget — must be created manually as part of landing zone setup.
Existing Subscriptions
Find gaps manually. Add missing budgets via Portal → Cost Management → Budgets.
